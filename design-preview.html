Option A — Alternating Side Panels

Protecting Forest Ecosystems

The Belize Maya Forest protection offers crucial ecological benefits including clean air, water, fertile soils, and essential pollinators while strengthening the regional Selva Maya Forest connectivity.

BMF Conservation Targets

Key conservation goals addressing deforestation, climate change, pesticide contamination, and invasive species — focusing on Forest Ecosystems, Aquatic Ecosystems, and Jaguars/Wildcats.

Protecting Biodiversity

A dedicated ranger team conducts regular patrols, deters illegal activities, and manages wildfire prevention across the 241,000-acre reserve.

Building Stewardship

Community stewardship programs empowering local communities to protect and sustainably manage the forest for generations to come.
